The band was formed in 1998 in Los angeles, California.

The alternative rock band currently has 4 studio albums and have sold over 10 million copies worldwide.

The band has been compared to other bands such as Pink Floyd, The cure, and U2 because of their philosphical and spirtual lyrics.

The name was taken from a rare manuscript 'Argus Apocraphex.'

Jared has stated that its a rough translation and a metaphor for the future.

Recorded the first album with Bob Ezrin( worked with Pink floyd, Kiss, and Alice cooper) and Brian Virtue. Before the album was completed the band went on tour with Puddle of Mudd.

Before recording the third album in 2009 the band tried to change record labels causing EMI to sue them for 30 million dollars. This supposedly inspired the title of the album 'This is War.'

The band released a documentry, directed by Jared, in 2012 called Artifact which followed the band through the lawsuit and recording.

Jared

Born 1971 in bossier city, lousianna.

Started his acting carrer in the 1990's.

Mostly known for his acting and musical carrer but he has also done directing and producing.

Music and Film:

Jared has described the music of the band as having the grandiosity of pink floyd and the energy of the sex pistols.

Jared has been described as a method actor he is known to take a role very seriously and has even risked his health by gaining and losing drastic amounts of weight for film's.

Jared's debut film was titled 'How to make an american quilt.'

Jared has gone back and forth between filiming and touring.

Recently while recording and touring for the fourth album 'Love, lust, faith, and dreams,' he also filmed 'Dallas buyers club' which among other awards he won an Oscar for best supporting actor.

Thomo

Tomo was born in Sarajevo in 1979.

His family moved to the US when he was baby.

After moving around for a few years his family settled in Michigan.

Tomo is not the only artist in the family his sister Ivana Milicevis is an actress and his uncle teaches music.

Tomo got married in 2011 to his long time girlfriend Vicki Bosanko.

While Tomo started playing music at a young age it wasnt until he was 17 when he discovered heavy metal that he started playing guitar up until then he practised to be a violinist.

Auditioned for the band after he graduated.

Shannen

Older brother of fellow band member Jared, shannen was born in Bossier city, Louisana in 1970.

Shannen started playing the drums from a young age.

Other drummers that Shannen look up to include Rodger Taylor of Queen.

Along with the drums Shannen also enjoys writing and photography.

Just like his younger brother Shannen also did some tv apperences in 'My so called life.'

The drum kit he uses has been customed made and includes over 400 pictures on the skin.

In interviews Shannen has stated he practices for 6 to 7 hours.
